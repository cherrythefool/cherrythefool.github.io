🚩 Minesweeper Treasure Hunt 💥

Story

You've spent decades decoding texts and consulting historians and experts, and you have finally deciphered the ancient, disintegrating map passed down from your great-grandparents. But the map leads to an old minefield...

Welcome, treasure hunter!
I'm protector of these lands, but the last time I've had a treasure hunter was here was before the war.
You're a welcome guest here, but be careful! I've made progress clearing away the ordnance left behind, but who knows? ...there's probably at least one mine left.
I'll do what I can to protect you but this is dangerous business!

How to play

Search the grid squares in the 3 meter by 3 meter area of the minefield below by clicking the area you think may have the treasure.
The protector of the land will mark neutral squares with a red flag after you've searched, indicating that there are no mines there.
Win the treasure (and the game!) by finding the square containing the treasure.
But click the mine...and it's game over.
If you search too long without finding anything, the protector will move you to a new field to prevent you from hitting a mine.
