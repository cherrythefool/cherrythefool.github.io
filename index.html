🚩 Minesweeper Treasure Hunt 💥

Story

You have been searching for gold with an ancient map which has been disintegrating. But the map leads to an old minefield...

Welcome, treasure hunter!
I'm glad you're here, the last treasure hunter to search this land has been out cold for a week with a concussion.
I thought we'd cleared those mines...
Luckily, there's only one area of the minefield that hasn't been searched.
I don't think there are any mines there, but who knows? Actually...there's probably at least one mine left.
Well, let's look for treasure!

How to play

Search the grid squares in the 3 meter by 3 meter area of the minefield below by clicking the square you think may have the treasure.
Win the treasure (and the game!) by finding the square containing TREASURE.
But click the mine...and it's game over.
